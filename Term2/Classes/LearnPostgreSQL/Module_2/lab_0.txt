Hello teacher and everyone.

In this module, we will learn about views in postgresql

This module includes 7 laboratory sessions that will help us understand 
how to create, manage, and optimize views in PostgreSQL, as well as 
explore ther underlying mechanisms.

Here are the seven laboratories:

Lab 1: Understanding what a view is and how it works

Lab 2: Creating and querying simple views 

Lab 3: Modifying and dropping views 

Lab 4: Updatable views and with check option 

Lab 5: Perfomance Considerations (Materialized vs Regular views)

Lab 6: Security and Acces Control Using Views

Lab 7: Advanced topics (Recursive views, Views chaining)
